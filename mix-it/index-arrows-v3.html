ARROWS GO!

Level 1

Pick an arrow from the spawn tray, tap an empty cell to place it. Arrows have different shapes and lengths — straight, L-bend.

Tap a placed arrow to release it. Path in front of the arrowhead must be fully clear.

Score = length × distance traveled. First to push the tug bar to their edge wins.
