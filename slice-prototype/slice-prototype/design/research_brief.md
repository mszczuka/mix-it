# Research brief

Date: 2026-06-26

**Slice to Meet You** (verified): Cozy idle/incremental slicing game. Developer/publisher
Lumamind Studio (Ukrainian indie). Premium, paid — $14.99 / 12.74 intro discount; released
24 June 2026 on Steam (PC). Core loop: slice ingredient → earn money → unlock produce/ingredients
→ unlock & upgrade blade collection → buy kitchen decorations granting speed/profit bonuses →
global speed/income upgrades. Knife-wear = light strategy: each blade has its own resource and
degrades, struggling with harder produce, pushing timely upgrades. Explicitly NO timers, NO
pressure. (Steam store page fetched 2026-06-26; corroborated dev.ua.)
Usage: feature/feel reference only; its premium no-monetization stance constrains TONE, not revenue.

**AdVenture Capitalist** (verified identity): F2P incremental, Hyper Hippo Productions, 2014
browser/Android, 2015 iOS/PC. >24mo old → structural claims evergreen, specific numbers labelled.
- Managers automate businesses, run profit cycles offline → turns clicker into idle.
- Angel Investors = permanent prestige currency on reset, global profit multiplier. "+2% per angel"
  reported by AdCap Wiki, not independently confirmed.
- Offline earnings accumulate while closed; monetization hybrid — rewarded video temp 2x + IAP gold
  for permanent multipliers/time warps (evergreen).
Usage: automation, prestige (angel), offline earnings, clean exponential curve. Drop pure-AFK.

**Comparable mobile F2P scanned:**
- Idle Slice and Dice (Google Play/App Store, GPP). Conveyor slicing factory. Ad-driven; per player
  reviews (not independently confirmed) ads excessive/forced, ad-removal behind weekly subscription.
  → Documented ANTI-PATTERN: what NOT to do to a cozy slicer.
- Idle benchmarks (benchmarks.md, "as of early 2025"): D1 30-40% / D7 15-22% / D30 7-12%; sessions
  2-5 min, 5-10/day; IAP conv 1.5-3%; ad rev 40-60%; rewarded eCPM $10-40, ~5-15 views/day before fatigue.

**Tension resolution (active-tapper-idle):** active tap-to-slice = meaningful multiplier-bearing verb;
automation (auto-slicers/managers) gradually absorbs grind à la AdCap; knife-wear keeps active play
strategic + is the soft-fail; offline earnings are the safety net, not the main engine.
