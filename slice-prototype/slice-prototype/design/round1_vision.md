# Round 1 — Vision

Date: 2026-06-26

## Pillar fantasy
The player feels like a satisfying knife master — a hypnotic, anti-stress
satisfaction of perfect slicing while the numbers grow with every slice.

## Reference games
- **Slice to Meet You** (Steam, idle/incremental)
  - ✅ Copy: tap-to-slice core verb, knife-wear management, blade collection tree,
    ingredient unlocks, kitchen decorations granting bonuses, cozy juice.
  - ❌ Drop: premium one-time ($14.99) business model.
- **AdVenture Capitalist** (idle)
  - ✅ Copy: idle automation, managers (auto-slicers), angel/prestige reset loop,
    clean exponential progression curve, offline earnings.
  - ❌ Drop: pure-AFK with zero active input (we keep active tapping).

## Audience + platform
- Mobile F2P (iOS/Android), portrait, one-thumb.
- Broad casual acquired via "satisfying slicing" creative; retained via idle/prestige meta.

## Hard constraints
- MUST: active tap-to-slice as the core verb; offline/idle earnings; knife-wear management.
- MUST-NOT: realtime PvP; complex tutorial; anything beyond one-thumb control.

## Tension to resolve (flagged to designer)
References lean AdCap (automation replaces tapping) but constraints require active
tapping. Resolution direction: **active-tapper idle** — the player actively slices,
while automation/managers gradually take over the grind (AdCap-style prestige).
Active tap stays meaningful (multiplier / combo / knife-wear), idle is the safety net.
