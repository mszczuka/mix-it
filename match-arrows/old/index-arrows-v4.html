ARROWS GO!

Level 1

Press SPAWN — a random arrow appears from the center, head pointing outward.

Tap a placed arrow to release it. Path in front of the head must be fully clear — sometimes arrows block each other and you must release them in the right order.

Score = length × distance. First to push the tug bar to their edge wins.
