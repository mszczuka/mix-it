ARROWS GO!

Level 1

One shared board. Each player gets 3 moves per turn.

Tap an arrow to release it. Only arrows that fully leave the board score points! Partially blocked arrows slide until they hit something and stay on the board — useful to open paths.

Flat points per tier (the higher the tier, the more blocked the arrow tends to spawn):
● common 1pt   ● rare 2pt   ● epic 3pt   ● legendary 5pt

CASCADE — releasing an arrow auto-fires those behind it on the same line, same direction, with ×2, ×3, ×4 multipliers.
STREAK — chain successful exits within a turn: ×2 boost, +1 move at ×3, double points at ×4, FIRESTORM ×3 at ×5.

After 3 minutes, the higher score wins.
